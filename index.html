Our Story

Iron & Oak started with one chair and a simple idea: treat every client like a regular. Over a decade later, we're proud to be the shop locals trust for a sharp cut and good conversation.

We blend classic barbering traditions with modern styles, so whether you want a timeless trim or the latest fade, you're in the right chair.

Questions?

Frequently Asked

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Both! We accept walk-ins when chairs are open, but booking ahead guarantees your spot and time.

How do I pay?

We accept cash and all major cards. Tips can be added in person.

What if I need to cancel?

No problem — just give us a call at least a couple hours ahead so we can offer the slot to someone else.

Do you cut kids' hair?

Absolutely. Kids cuts are $20 and our barbers are great with little ones.
